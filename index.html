The Perseid meteor shower

•The Perseid meteor shower, its bright meteors and high frequency, it peaks in mid-August. Find a Dark Location: The best views are far from city lights, which can obscure the meteors.
•The Perseid meteor shower, commonly known as the Perseids, is visible annually from mid-July to late August.
•Although the moon will be 50% illuminated during the peak of the Perseids this year, it will set around midnight, providing dark skies until dawn — ideal for meteor watching!
